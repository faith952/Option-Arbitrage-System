# Reading Plan

## Current Goal

Build a 2-3 month prototype of an option research and arbitrage system, while deepening understanding of futures, options, pricing, volatility, trading, and risk management.

## Core Books

1. Hull - Options, Futures, and Other Derivatives
2. Natenberg - Option Volatility and Pricing
3. Trading Options Greeks
4. Euan Sinclair - Volatility Trading
5. Commodity Investing and Trading
6. The Handbook of Commodity Investing

## Current Stage: Week 1

Topic: Option Basics and Payoff Structures

Reading Focus:
- Option buyer and seller
- Call and Put
- Premium
- Payoff and profit
- Linear vs nonlinear risk
- Futures vs options

Key Questions:
1. Why do options exist?
2. Why is option risk nonlinear?
3. Why is premium not just a fee?
4. Why can options be used for industrial hedging?
5. Why is selling options dangerous despite high win rate?

## Rule

Do not read books randomly.
Each reading task must connect to one notebook, one docs note, or one market/industry question.
